Founders of the Agency

ALEXANDER KERIMOV

MANAGING PARTNER

Alexander graduated from the University of International Business (UIB) in 2013 with a degree in Journalism and PR. Holds international certificates and diplomas in the field of PR.

Since 2008, he has been the founder of the DRIVE2.ru community in Kazakhstan. From 2011 to 2013, he worked as a journalist in automotive media and led the social direction of the Lafa.kz portal (later Motor.kz).

Worked in local and network PR agencies, has experience in SMM - worked in the finance, automotive, and IT sectors. Since August 2015, he has been a Managing Partner of the Grey Cardinals agency.

OLGA KALENICHUK

MANAGING PARTNER

In 2009, Olga graduated from KazEU named after Ryskulov with a degree in International Relations. In 2013, she completed her master's degree at the Austrian business school Lauder Business School.

She began her professional career in 2007 in the largest banks of Kazakhstan, in 2010 she headed the editorial office of the well-known magazine Advertising.

Since 2011, she has worked in Europe and Kazakhstan in network and local agencies with international clients, and in 2015, together with Alexander, they founded the Grey Cardinals agency.
