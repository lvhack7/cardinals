Alice in
Kazakh Language

Alice has started understanding and speaking Kazakh. This is the first step towards developing a virtual assistant in the region.

Task

PR Campaign Objective: Increase awareness among the population of Kazakhstan about the unique capabilities of Yandex Alice in Kazakh language, draw attention to the learning opportunities with the virtual assistant according to individual user preferences.

  • Attract attention of media, influencers, bloggers to the virtual assistant
  • Fill the information field in Kazakhstan with positive messages about Alice's new capabilities
  • Develop communication and image strategy for Yandex Kazakhstan

Special Projects

Special Project on Nurkz
One of the key projects in support of launching Alice in Kazakh language was a special project on the Nur.kz platform in collaboration with popular influencer, chemistry teacher from Almaty, and participant of the "30 under 30" Forbes Kazakhstan rating - Ulan Usenov.
Ulan visited the Yandex office, where he was greeted by Yandex Search manager - Saltanat Bolatbekova.
He shared with the editorial team how digital technologies are currently impacting children, how to protect children from unwanted content, and also shared his opinion on the future development of technologies in education.
Additionally, he was able to test the voice assistant "Alice" in Kazakh language at the office. (source)

Special Project on Tengrinews
Yandex company employees shared details of creating services and digital solutions aimed at supporting Kazakhs in their efforts to study and develop their native language.
For example, to implement the quick search feature, Yandex Kazakhstan processed Kazakh-language content using neural network technologies and machine learning methods.
This experience is unique because no other search engine in the world provides quick answers in Kazakh language, considering that this language does not meet the criteria of most companies creating products in foreign languages. Additionally, most audio and text products and technologies are oriented towards popular languages.
(source)

Special Project on TheVillage
In the special project implemented jointly with The Village media, we learned about the life of a student at the journalism faculty.

17-year-old student Samat Adilov was invited to participate in the special project. He told us about his choice of profession, university, shared about life and studies at the university. In addition, Samat talked about the technologies he uses during his studies and how they simplify his access to necessary information.
The use of artificial intelligence has become an integral part of a student's life. Moreover, the information needed for study must be in Kazakh language, and in this matter, Yandex helps him with the quick answer function in Kazakh language. (source)

Radio and TV Broadcasts

Broadcast on radio "Dacha Kazakhstan" and radio "Zhuldyz FM"
Coverage of an information event on two leading radio stations: Radio Dacha and Radio Zhuldyz. In addition to this, radio presenters had a conversation with Alice. They found out today's weather in Almaty, asked for a recipe for beshbarmak, clarified the height of Khan-Tengri peak, and asked Alice to tell a joke.

TV segment placement on Eurasia Channel 1
Journalists from the "First Channel Eurasia" visited the Yandex office. Saltanat Bolatbekova - Product Owner Yandex Search Kazakhstan talked about the launch of Alice in the mobile application in Kazakh language. Also, Nargiza Myrzabekova, a student at AlmaU University, Faculty of Journalism and Reporting, took part in the segment. Nargiza shared how she uses various technologies that help in her studies."
